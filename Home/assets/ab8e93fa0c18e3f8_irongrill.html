      FAQs

      help

      How does the electric grill brush work?

      It uses a motorized rotating head that spins the bristles at high speed to break down grease, burnt food, and carbon buildup with minimal effort.

      help

      Is it safe to use on all grill types?

      Yes, it’s designed to be safe on most grill grates including stainless steel and cast iron. Always avoid using on delicate non-stick coatings unless specified by the manufacturer.

      help

      Do I need to scrub hard?

      No. The electric motor does the heavy lifting. You simply guide the brush over the grates while it cleans.

      help

      Is it rechargeable or battery-powered?

      Yes, it’s rechargeable for cordless convenience, making it easy to use anywhere without being tied to an outlet.

      help

      How do I clean the brush itself?

      After use, rinse or wipe the brush head to remove debris and let it dry before storing.
